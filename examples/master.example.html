GALREY

A gentle introduction

Galrey is a presentation system entirely built on a CSS(3), HTML(5) and JavaScript foundation.

It has been inspired by S5 of Eric Meyer fame and the html5rocks website.

Please use Chrome or Safari for a better experience: Galrey itself is being developed with a top down approach: first make it works with better browsers (Webkit based) and eventually every feature will be ported to the less fancy browsers.

Now press →, D, or just swipe right (h for help)

What Galrey gives you

  • A robust and straightforward HTML5 "master" file to insert your slides into
  • A simple and extensibile way to add your own CSS (think «theming»)
  • Keyboard and touch slides navigation (next, prev, first, etc)
  • Auto or fixed viewport resizing
  • Unobtrusive enhancement (look: no scripts! No styles!)
  • Works offline
  • (continue...)

What Galrey gives you – (continued)

  • Autoplay
  • Nifty transitions and animations (via CSS3)
  • Sticky header and footer
  • On screen help (just press ?)
  • Audio and Video HTML5 elements (with smart start/stop)
  • Audio volume timed decrease
  • Good printing support
  • jQuery goodness
  • Every slide is bookmarkable

What Galrey WILL give you

  • Better tablets/iOS integration
  • A CSS grid framework for better element placing
  • Other timed goodies
  • Support for Firefox and IE9 (and Opera as well)
  • Better printing support
  • Custom animations/plugins
  • Remotely controlled synchronous slideshows

A slide it's simply an <article>

                    <article>
                    
                      <section>
                      
                        <h1>A slide it's simply an <article></h1>
                        
                        <pre>...</pre>
                        
                      </section>
                      
                    </article>

The article and section elements are mandatory.

You can then put whatever you like into the section child.

The master file

<!DOCTYPE html> 
<html>
  <head>
    <meta charset="utf-8">
    <title>My presentation</title>
    <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="galrey/themes/default.css">
    <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="theme.css">
    <script src="galrey/bootstrap.js"></script>
  </head>
  <body>
    <div id="galrey">

      <header>My header</header>
      <footer>My footer</footer>

      <div class="slides">

        <article>
          <section>
            <h1>Slide title</h1>
            <p>A Paragraph</p>
          </section>
        </article>

        <article>
          <section>
            <h1>Another slide title</h1>
            <p>Another paragraph</p>
          </section>
        </article>
      </div>

    </div>

  </body>

</html>
